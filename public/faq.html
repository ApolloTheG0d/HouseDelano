Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions our customers ask about our pet services.

1. What are your business hours?

We’re open Monday through Saturday from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM. Sunday is by appointment only.

2. Do I need to bring vaccination records?

Yes, all pets must have up-to-date vaccination records for their safety and the safety of others.

3. What should I pack for boarding?

Please bring your pet’s food, medication (if needed), and a favorite toy or blanket to help them feel at home.

4. Can I check on my pet while I’m away?

Of course! You can call us anytime during business hours or request photo updates by email.

5. What if my pet has special medical needs?

We’re trained to administer medication and handle pets with special requirements. Please discuss this when booking.

6. Do you offer grooming for all breeds?

Yes, our grooming services are open to all breeds of dogs and cats. Prices may vary depending on coat type and size.

7. How do I schedule a Meet & Greet?

Visit our Meet & Greet page to fill out a simple form and pick a convenient time.
