About House Delano

Founded in 2020, we provide safe, loving, and reliable care for the pets in our community.

Our Story

What began as a small, family-run endeavor has grown into a full-service hub for boarding, daycare, and grooming, without losing the warmth and personal touch that pets (and their people) love.

Our Mission

Every pet deserves comfort, care, and companionship while away from home. We combine professional training with a calm, enriching environment so pets feel safe and engaged.

Our Facility

  • Separate, species-specific spaces for dogs and cats
  • 24/7 monitoring and medication administration as needed
  • Play zones for social butterflies and quiet suites for solo chillers
